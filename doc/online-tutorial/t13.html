We can run a gapfilling command on our model to automatically add reactions as needed to enable the production of all biomass components: 
  gapfill_fbamodel < initial.model > gapfilled.model

 

Depending on the size and state of the genome, this could take minutes to hours, but when the analysis is complete, your model will have additional reactions in it, reflecting the ideal solution identified by the gapfilling algorithm to enable growth. Now we can rerun our flux balance analysis to determine the biomass production pathways of our organism.
